About this place

Enjoy your own private bedroom with private bathroom within a nice quiet neighborhood located just 2 short miles from town with bus transit or sea-side walking/ bike path access. Come relax in a beautiful quiet setting with ocean view and Ketchikan attractions close by.The spaceThe bedroom is in a 3 bedroom duplex shared with the host Lena and yourself. You will have your own private bedroom and personal bathroom. Kitchen and dinning space are shared.Guest accessNice quiet neighborhood2 miles to Coast Guard base and townSea-side path to walk or bicycle into town. This place is located near by to down town (approximately 5 minutes to drive) where you can visit historical museums and explore the heritage of Ketchikan, known as the First City. You’ll have your own private bedroom and bathroom, and washer and dryer also available. Kitchen space is shared. Coffee and tea are complementary.During your stayI am more than happy to speak with guests and assist when available.
