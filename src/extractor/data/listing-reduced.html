Only steps from the beach, this ocean view unit has everything a family would need for a relaxing summer vacation.The spaceThe large private balcony with outdoor furniture is the ideal place to relax and sip your morning coffee while watching the sunrise over the ocean. An equipped kitchen with full sized fridge, stove and microwave, all cooking utensils and dinnerware supplied. Bathroom has a toilet and a shower/tub combination. There are two smart Tv's in the unit and two queen beds with the best ocean view. This condo is located at the Villas (part of the Bluewater Resort oceanfront resort). Guests have access to all of the Bluewater Resorts amenities during the stay.Guest accessBluewater resort is an oceanfront complex with individually owned condominiums. The Bluewater consists of a main tower (14 stories on ocean side) and two Villas (4 storey- across the road). As our guests, you have access to all the resorts amenities. This includes free secured parking, free resort wi-fi, multiple pools (indoor and outdoor, from 2ft to 8ft deep, small lazy river at Villas), hot tubs (indoor and outdoor), gym, sauna, arcade/games room and onsite restaurant/bar (Pizza/Italian) and outside tiki bar. An onsite laundry is available for all our guests.Other things to noteAll Linens and bath towels are provided with this rental unit. This unit is professionally cleaned, and all linens are commercially laundered after each stay.Virtual check-in process with unique door code emailed to guest prior to arrival. There is NO front desk check in or concierge services. This is an individually owned unit and not a hotel room. We are unable to provide daily maid service or replenish towels during your stay. Your room will have one bath towel per person. We do not provide beach towels – so please bring your own.There is an onsite, coin operated laundry room on-site. There is also a change machine.We do provide a basic starter kit of necessities for your stay (2 rolls of toilet paper per bathroom, 1 x shampoo/conditioner/soap personal size, 1 container of hand soap per bathroom, 1 roll of paper towels, 5 trash bags, dishwasher pods, dish detergent, dish towels and wash rags.
