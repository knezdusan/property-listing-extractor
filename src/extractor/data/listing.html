About this place

On the North West Coast of BC, called Oona River (only accessible by seaplane and boat), we salvaged a part of a fishing lodges accommodations that was built on a barge as it became grounded. We landed this “barge” and have transformed the lower part into a common area with kitchen, living room with TV and games. The 2nd floor offers a private queen bed room with its own full bathroom including a shower. Take a hike, comb the beach at low tide, have a beach fire or relax on the patio.
